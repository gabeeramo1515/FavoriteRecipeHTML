Strawberry Smoothie :)

Enjoy drinking nutritious smoothies from time to time. My favorite being Strawberry flavored.

  • Prep: 5 Minutes
  • Ready In: 6 minutes

Ingredients:

  • 8 Strawberries, hulled: $1.98
  • 1/2 Cup of Skim Milk: $2.95
  • 3 Tablespoons of White Sugar: $1.37
  • 6 Ice Cubes, crushed: Should be free
  • 2 Teaspoons of Vanilla Extract: $4.12
  • 1/2 Cup of Plain Yogurt: $1.84

Super Difficult Directions:

  1. In a blender combine strawberries, milk, yogurt, sugar, and vanilla. Toss in the ice. Blend it until smooth and creamy.
    Pour into glasses and serve.
  2. Enjoy a phenomenal tasting smoothie.

Notes:

Feel free to use absolutely anyhting you want to hold your new refreshing beverage! This could even be your hands!
